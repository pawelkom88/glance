# Documentation | Glance — Teleprompter Overlay

## User Guide & Documentation

Learn how to present with confidence. From installation to section-jump workflows.

## Introduction

Glance is an elegant, transparent teleprompter overlay for macOS and Windows. Designed for professionals who need to maintain perfect eye contact during high-stakes Zoom, Teams, or Google Meet calls.

**Privacy First**  
Glance operates entirely locally. Your scripts never leave your device, and we never track your usage. No account, no cloud, no telemetry.

## Installation

### macOS

1. Download the `.dmg` installer.
2. Drag **Glance** to your Applications folder.
3. Open the app, then go to **System Settings** → **Privacy & Security** and confirm under Security.

### Windows

Glance is available directly in the **Microsoft Store** for seamless setup.

1. Search for "Glance Teleprompter" in the Store.
2. Click **Install**.
3. Launch from your Start Menu.

## The Onboarding Flow

Glance is designed to be learned in seconds. When you first launch the app, follow these three steps:

- **Prepare your script:** Paste or import your text directly or drop a Markdown file into the editor.
- **Automatic Positioning:** The prompter launches at the top center under your camera. You can adjust the height or collapse controls to fit your setup.
- **Control your flow:** Adjust speed and font size on the fly, or hide the prompter instantly using keyboard shortcuts.

## Managing Scripts

You can work with scripts in three ways:

- **Direct Entry:** Type and edit directly within Glance.
- **Paste:** Bring in text from any document or website.
- **Markdown Import:** Load `.md` files to keep your styling and headings intact.

## Presentation Controls

Customize your reading experience on the fly. Glance puts all the essential controls directly at your fingertips.

### Play & Pause
Start or stop the prompter scroll instantly. Perfect for pausing to expand on a thought or answer a question.

### Adjust Speed
Fine-tune the scrolling pace to match your natural speaking rhythm using the slider or keyboard shortcuts.

### Font Size
Dial in the perfect text scale. Make your script readable at any distance without squinting or leaning in.

### Toggle Panel
Once your settings are dialled in, hide the controls completely to enjoy a distraction-free confidence screen.

### Snap to Center
Instantly align your text directly under your camera lens to guarantee flawless, natural eye contact with your audience.

### Adjust Opacity
Dim the prompter background so it blends seamlessly into your screen, or make it fully opaque for maximum readability.

### Elapsed Timer
Keep track of your presentation length in real-time. Ensure you hit your marks and never accidentally run over time.

### Quick Hide
Need to check your notes or a window beneath the prompter? Hide the entire overlay instantly when it's not needed.

## Keyboard Shortcuts

Control your presentation flow without ever touching the mouse.

- Toggle Prompter: Switch between Editor and Prompt
- Increase Speed: Speed up the text scroll
- Decrease Speed: Slow down the text scroll
- Font Size Up: Make the text larger
- Font Size Down: Make the text smaller
- Toggle Opacity: Adjust window transparency

## Power Tip: Section Jumps

In high-stakes calls, conversations are rarely linear. If you use Markdown headings (`#`, `##`), Glance automatically creates valid jump points.

Press **Ctrl + [Number]** to jump instantly to that section. Perfect for jumping back to a specific talking point during a Q&A session.

## Reliability & Security

Because Glance is a local desktop application, it works perfectly even if your internet connection drops. More importantly, your business-critical scripts, sales demos, and confidential notes never touch a server.
