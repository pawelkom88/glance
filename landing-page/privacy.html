Privacy Policy

Last Updated: February 2026

Glance was built with a core philosophy: your words belong to you. Because Glance is designed to help you read your private notes, scripts, and presentations, we've built the app to be completely local and private by design.

1. Zero Data Collection

We do not collect, store, or transmit any personal information, usage data, or analytics. Glance does not have user accounts, and there is no login required.

2. Local Processing Only

All of your Markdown files, notes, and preferences are processed and stored exclusively on your local device. Glance never uploads your documents to the cloud or syncs them to any external servers.

3. No Telemetry or Tracking

We do not embed any analytics SDKs, crash reporters, or telemetry tools inside the Glance application. What you do inside the app is entirely your business.

4. Network Requests

Glance operates entirely offline. The only time the app interacts with the internet is if you manually click on external links (such as the "Buy me a coffee" link), which will open in your default web browser.

5. Changes to this Policy

If we ever decide to introduce optional features that require network access (such as automatic software updates), this policy will be explicitly updated, and those features will always be strictly opt-in.
