## Install Help

### Not distributed via the App Store

Glance is distributed directly from this website. Your OS may ask for a one-time security confirmation — this is expected and safe. You can revert this setting at any time.

### Open Glance safely

Choose your platform and follow the built-in OS prompts. You only need to do this once.

#### macOS

1. **Open Glance once** from Applications or the downloaded DMG.
2. If macOS blocks it, open **System Settings → Privacy & Security**.
3. In the Security section, click **Open Anyway** for Glance.
4. Confirm **Open**, then authenticate with your password or Touch ID.

#### Windows

1. **Run the downloaded installer** from this website.
2. If SmartScreen appears with “Windows protected your PC,” click **More info**.
3. Choose **Run anyway** only if the file came from the official Glance download link.
4. Keep **App & browser control** protections enabled after install.

Tip: This prompt is expected for apps distributed directly outside the Mac App Store or Microsoft Store.

## Frequently asked

### Is Glance visible to other people on the call?
No. Glance is a local overlay that never enters your camera feed. One tip: if you choose to share your **entire screen**, the prompter will be visible to others. To keep your script private, always share only the specific **application window** or **tab** you are presenting.

### Does Glance work with Zoom, Teams, and Google Meet?
Yes. Glance works as a topmost overlay, meaning it sits above any video conferencing app you use.

### Does Glance record my screen, camera, or audio?
Never. Glance has no recording capabilities. It is a purely visual aid for your own use.

### Can I change scroll speed and font size?
Yes. You can adjust all of these settings on the fly using keyboard shortcuts or the settings menu.

### Do I need an account?
No account required. Glance is a standalone application that runs entirely on your local machine.

### Is my script stored in the cloud?
No. Your scripts stay on your device. Glance does not have a cloud component and does not upload your data anywhere.

### Does it work offline?
Yes. Once downloaded, Glance works perfectly without an internet connection.

### Is there a Windows version?
Yes, Glance is available for both macOS and Windows.
