# Privacy Policy

Last Updated: March 2026

Glance was built with a core philosophy: **your words belong to you.** Because Glance is designed to help you read your private notes, scripts, and presentations, we've built the app to be completely local and private by design. While our website uses basic, privacy-friendly analytics to help us understand how people find us, your work within the app never leaves your device.

**Data Controller:** Glance is operated by Glance team, based in the United Kingdom. This policy is compliant with the UK GDPR. For any privacy-related questions, contact us at [hello@atglance.app](mailto:hello@atglance.app).

## 1. Zero In-App Data Collection

We do not collect, store, or transmit any personal information, usage data, or scripts from within the Glance application. Glance does not have user accounts, and there is no login required.

## 2. Local Processing Only

All of your Markdown files, notes, and preferences are processed and stored exclusively on your local device. Glance never uploads your documents to the cloud or syncs them to any external servers.

## 3. Website Analytics

This website uses Mixpanel to help us understand discovery and download trends, but only with your explicit consent. When you accept cookies, Mixpanel collects the following data about your visit:

- Pages visited and time of visit
- Download button clicks (platform: macOS or Windows)
- General browser and device type (no precise fingerprinting)
- Anonymised IP address (used to derive approximate country only)

This data is retained by Mixpanel for up to 90 days. No personal data is collected and no data is linked to any individual. To withdraw consent at any time, clear your browser's local storage for this site (the preference is stored under the key `glance_cookie_consent`). Declining analytics in the cookie banner means Mixpanel is never loaded.

No telemetry or tracking exists within the Glance desktop application itself.

## 4. Network Requests

The Glance application operates entirely offline. It does not make network requests unless you manually click on external support links, which open in your default web browser.

## 5. Not Affiliated

Glance is an independent tool and is not affiliated with, sponsored by, or endorsed by Zoom Video Communications, Inc., Microsoft Corporation, or Google LLC.

## 6. Changes to this Policy

If we ever decide to introduce optional features that require network access, this policy will be explicitly updated, and those features will always be strictly opt-in.

## 7. Contact

For any privacy-related questions or requests, contact: [hello@atglance.app](mailto:hello@atglance.app)
